These are different types of baits that fish tend to go for:

Oftentimes called Ika, squid is an excellent bait you can throw on your hook that predatory fish just love.

Some people's favorite sea foods are a an excellent bait to use when fishing for reef fish.

Believe it or not, a lot of fish will go after white bread on a hook, especially the opportunistic ones.

Some fish such as Nenue, also called the gray chub, love the taste of seaweed over proteins like ika or shrimp.
